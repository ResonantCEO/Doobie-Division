
# Analytics and Reports Options

Here are 40 analytics and report options that could be incorporated into the design:

• **Total Revenue**: Overall income generated in a specified period.

• **Total Expenses**: Total costs incurred during a specific timeframe.

• **Net Profit**: Revenue minus expenses over a defined period.

• **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to acquire a new customer.

• **Customer Retention Rate**: Percentage of customers retained over a time period.

• **Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers lost during a given timeframe.

• **Sales Growth Rate**: Increase in sales over specific periods.

• **Average Transaction Value**: Average amount spent per transaction.

• **Sales by Product Category**: Breakdown of sales by product categories.

• **Most Popular Products**: Products with the highest sales volume.

• **Least Popular Products**: Products with the lowest sales volume.

• **Order Fulfillment Time**: Average time taken to fulfill orders.

• **Return Rate**: Percentage of orders that are returned.

• **Sales per Channel**: Revenue generated from different sales channels (e.g., online, in-store).

• **Time of Purchase Analysis**: Peak times when purchases are made.

• **Demographic Insights**: Customer breakdown by age, gender, location, etc.

• **Abandoned Cart Rate**: Percentage of initiated purchases that were not completed.

• **Product Inventory Levels**: Current stock levels for each product.

• **Stock Turnover Rate**: Frequency of stock replenishment.

• **Total Active Customers**: Number of customers making purchases within a specified period.

• **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Estimated revenue from a customer over their entire relationship with the business.

• **Market Segment Performance**: Analysis of sales by different customer segments.

• **Email Campaign Effectiveness**: Metrics on open rates, click rates, and conversions from email campaigns.

• **Social Media Engagement**: Engagement metrics from social media campaigns.

• **Website Traffic**: Number of visitors to the website over time.

• **Conversion Rates by Traffic Source**: Analysis of conversion rates based on where traffic originates.

• **Seasonal Sales Trends**: Sales performance comparison across different seasons.

• **Customer Feedback and Satisfaction**: Insights from customer reviews and surveys.

• **Sales Forecasting**: Predictive analytics on future sales trends.

• **Supplier Performance Metrics**: Analysis of on-time deliveries and stock quality.

• **Cross-Sell and Upsell Success Rates**: Metrics on additional sales initiated during transactions.

• **Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**: Total direct costs attributable to product sales.

• **Sales Attribution Analysis**: Understanding which marketing strategies contributed to sales.

• **Inventory Aging Report**: Age of inventory items to manage stock levels effectively.

• **Operational Efficiency Metrics**: Time and cost taken to process orders and complete deliveries.

• **Sales Penetration Rates**: Proportion of sales compared to target markets.

• **Customer Segmentation Report**: Breakdown of customers into various segments for targeted marketing.

• **Click-Through Rates (CTR)**: Measure of how often people click on marketing materials.

• **Campaign ROI**: Return on investment for specific marketing campaigns.

• **Market Share Analysis**: Understanding how the business stands relative to competitors.
